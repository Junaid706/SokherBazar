Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about SokherBazar.

General Questions

What is SokherBazar?

SokherBazar is an online marketplace where you can buy authentic handmade products from rural artisans across Bangladesh.

How does SokherBazar support artisans?

The platform allows artisans to open shops, showcase their products, share their stories, and sell directly to buyers while building trust and visibility.

Is SokherBazar safe for online shopping?

Yes. All transactions are secure, sellers are verified, and the platform has a review system to ensure product quality.

Can I contact an artisan directly?

Yes. Buyers can contact artisans via the messaging system on the dashboard for questions about products or customization.

Buyer Questions

How do I create an account?

You can register using the "Register" link in the header, select your account type (Buyer), and fill in your details.

How can I track my orders?

Once your order is placed, you can track it in your dashboard under "Order History" or receive updates via email/SMS notifications.

Can I return or exchange a product?

Return and exchange policies depend on the seller. Please check the product page or contact the artisan through your dashboard.

What payment methods are available?

SokherBazar supports multiple secure payment options, including online banking, mobile payment apps, and cash on delivery (where available).

Seller Questions

How do I open a shop?

Artisans can register with SokherBazar, submit verification documents, and after approval, create and manage their shop and product listings.

Can I share my story on SokherBazar?

Yes. You can request to be featured in "Seller Story" videos, which showcase your craft and journey to buyers.

How can I manage orders?

Sellers can view orders in their dashboard, update delivery status, and communicate with buyers if needed.

Do I get analytics for my shop?

Yes. Sellers have access to basic analytics like product views, order count, and earnings to improve their shop performance.

Shipping & Delivery

Does SokherBazar handle delivery?

The platform partners with courier services for secure delivery. Sellers prepare the order, and the courier delivers it to the buyer.

Can I change the shipping address after ordering?

Shipping details can be updated only before the order is processed. Contact support immediately if changes are needed.

Support

How can I contact SokherBazar support?

You can reach us via the Contact page or email at support@sokherbazar.com.

Can I report a problem with a product or seller?

Yes. Use the "Report" option on the product page or contact support to resolve issues.

Does SokherBazar have notifications for offers and updates?

Yes. Buyers and sellers receive notifications for promotions, order updates, and featured artisan stories.
