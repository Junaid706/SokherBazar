Our Mission

To preserve traditional craftsmanship and create sustainable income for rural artisans by connecting them to modern markets.

We showcase carefully verified, hand-made goods — each product carries a story. We focus on fairness, trust, and accessibility so artisans get a fair share.

By building trust (reviews, verification, seller story videos) and providing a simple Bangla-first UI, we make e-commerce accessible to everyone.
